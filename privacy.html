Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 15, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the “Automation” web application (“we”, “us”, “our”), developed and operated as an individual project. Our app lets users automate Instagram DMs and comment replies through the Instagram API for marketing, customer support, and engagement purposes.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the most recent chat messages from connected Instagram accounts for the purpose of automation. No personal user information (such as Instagram username, profile picture, or other identifying details) is stored or processed by our service.

3. Use of Information

Collected chat messages are solely used to remember the chat context and to provide better automated responses within your Instagram account. We do not use this data for any other purpose.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any collected information with third parties or external services, except for integration with OpenAI for AI-powered features. All shared data is limited strictly to what is necessary for enabling AI responses, and no personal information or Instagram credentials are shared.

5. Data Storage and Security

Only a limited number of recent chat messages are stored, excluding any identifying information. All stored data is kept securely and protected from unauthorized access.

6. Data Deletion

Users can delete their account or revoke access at any time, upon which all stored chat messages associated with their account will be permanently deleted from our system.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Instagram Graph API (for automation features)
  • OpenAI (for AI-powered response generation)

8. Children’s Privacy

Our app is intended for users who already use Instagram and is not designed for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
